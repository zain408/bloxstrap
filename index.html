      • Features of Bloxstrap

      Bloxstrap is packed with features that make it stand out from other bootstrappers. Here are some of the key functionalities that Bloxstrap offers:

      Fast Flag Editing

      FastFlags are hidden settings within Roblox that control experimental or advanced features. With Bloxstrap, you can easily enable or disable these flags to enhance your gaming or testing experience. This feature is a boon for developers and power users seeking to explore Roblox's capabilities.

      File Modifications

      Tired of your custom settings being overwritten by Roblox updates? Bloxstrap ensures that your modified files, such as themes or sound changes, remain intact even after updates. It offers a reliable way to keep your preferences consistent.

      Activity Tracking

      Keep track of your in-game activity with this powerful feature. Whether you're monitoring game progress, checking session details, or evaluating your performance, Bloxstrap provides the tools to better understand and enjoy your gaming time.

      Discord Rich Presence

      Love showcasing your Roblox activity to friends on Discord? Bloxstrap seamlessly integrates Discord Rich Presence, allowing your friends to see what game you're playing, your progress, and even join your session directly from Discord. You can customize the information displayed, making your gaming sessions more social and interactive.

      Custom Mods

      With Bloxstrap, you can apply mods to Roblox effortlessly. From custom sounds (like the nostalgic old death sound) to fonts and even cursors, you can tailor every aspect of the game to your liking. The app also supports user-created mods, so you can push the boundaries of customization.

      Custom Mods

      Bloxstrap allows you to completely transform the visual appearance of your Roblox client. With custom themes, you can pick colors, fonts, and designs that reflect your style, making your gaming interface truly yours. Say goodbye to the default look and feel of Roblox and step into a more personalized gaming world.

      Customizable Launcher

      The launcher is the central hub of your Roblox experience. Bloxstrap offers a user-friendly and highly customizable launcher that lets you tweak settings, apply updates, and manage configurations—all in one place. Its intuitive design makes it easy for both beginners and advanced users to navigate

      Region Checker

      Bloxstrap includes a region checker to identify which Roblox server region you’re connected to. It also allows you to filter or switch regions, helping you find servers with the best latency for smoother gameplay and less lag.

      Multiple Instances

      Want to run more than one Roblox game at a time? Bloxstrap lets you do just that. This feature is especially useful for developers testing games or players who want to enjoy different game modes simultaneously without interruptions.
