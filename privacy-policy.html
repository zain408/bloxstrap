Usage Data

Usage Data is collected automatically when using the Service.

Usage Data may include information such as your Device's Internet Protocol address (e.g., IP address), browser type, browser version, our Service pages that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, unique device identifiers, and other diagnostic data.

We use Cookies and similar tracking technologies to track the activity on Our Service and store certain information. The technologies we use may include:

Certain sections of our Service and our emails may contain small electronic files known as web beacons (also referred to as clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs) that permit the Company, for example, to count users who have visited those pages or to gather other related website statistics (for example, recording the popularity of a certain section and verifying system and server integrity).

For more detailed information on the cookies we use and your choices regarding cookies, please visit our Cookies Policy.
